About me


In 2001 I began practicing yoga asana and pranayama (pose and breath work) and I recall seeing my worries leave my body and rise up to the ceiling in little bubbles. I began to release my burdons. In 2010 I completed my 200 hour yoga teacher training and in 2015 I completed my 500 hour training with over 4000 classes taught to date. I went on to become certified as an Integrative and Holistic Life Coach in 2017 with a focus on intuitive healing through meditation.



We offer classes that are suitable to absolute beginners to more advance students. Each class is unique as we practice different Kriyas (sets) each lesson.

Kundalini Yoga

Kundalini Yoga classes consist of simple yoga techniques that can be enjoyed by everyone. Classes consist of breath work, yoga postures, sound and chanting as well as meditation.
